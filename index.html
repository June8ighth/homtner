Your At-Home Workout Partner

Download the official HOMTNER app for Android. Plan a workout that suits you and follow it regularly to achieve health and a great body. AI trainer will be with your training.

Features

Personalized Workout Plan

What kind of workout do you do? How many sets? How long will you take a break? … Just work out according to your plan.

At-Home Workout

You can work out yourself regularly without a professional trainer and expensive workout program.

Powered by AI(Artificial Intelligence)

The AI trainer recognizes and tracks your posture through the camera. And judge the completion of correct posture.

Pose Estimation

Using a deep learning model, the joint is detected from the image captured from the camera and the posture is estimated based on the position of the joints.
